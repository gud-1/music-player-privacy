# Privacy Policy for Music Player by gud

**Last updated: July 14, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how "gud" ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our Music Player mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Personal Data
The App is designed to be a local music player. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII). You are not required to create an account or provide any information to use the App.

### Local Media Files
The App requires access to your device's local storage (specifically audio files) to function. This access is used solely to:
*   Scan for audio files on your device.
*   Display and play your music.
*   Manage playlists and favorites within the App's local database.

Your music files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

## 2. Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the App requests the following permissions:
*   **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO**: Required to access and play audio files stored on your device.
*   **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: Required to show playback controls in the notification area.
*   **FOREGROUND_SERVICE**: Required to keep the music playing while the App is in the background.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party libraries to enhance your experience:
*   **Glide**: Used for loading and caching album art.
*   **Android Jetpack Libraries**: Used for core App functionality and architecture.

These libraries do not collect any personal information from you through our App.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
