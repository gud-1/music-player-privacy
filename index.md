# Privacy Policy for Music Player

**Effective Date:** July 14, 2026
**Owner:** gud

This Privacy Policy describes how "Music Player" (the "App"), developed by **gud**, handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and have designed this App to be as private and secure as possible.

### 1. No Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
The App is designed to be a local-only music player. We do not have servers, and we do not track your usage, your location, or your identity.

### 2. Permissions and Their Use
The App requires specific permissions to function. Here is why we need them:
- **Storage/Media Audio Permission:** Required to scan your device for local music files and play them. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded.
- **Notification Permission:** Required to show playback controls (Play/Pause/Skip) in your device's notification area.

### 3. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party libraries for better performance:
- **Glide:** Used to display album art from your local music files. Glide does not collect any personal data through our App.
- **Android Support/Jetpack Libraries:** Standard Google libraries used for the App's interface and navigation.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Since we do not collect any data, the App is safe for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your email, we recommend checking this page for any changes.
